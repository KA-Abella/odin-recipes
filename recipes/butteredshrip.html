Buttered Shrimp Dish

Description:

It is simply made of Shrimp, garlic, butter, and lemon soda. Donâ€™t take the lemon soda for granted because it is a major ingredient. It refers to soda pop such as Sprite, 7Up, Sierra Mist, and the likes. The sweet taste of this dish is attributed to this ingredient. It also blends well with butter and garlic.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs shrimp cleaned
  • 2 tablespoons parsley chopped
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 head garlic crushed
  • 1 cup lemon lime soda
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Start by cleaning the shrimp. Wash it thoroughly and cut the antennae as much as possible.
  2. Devein each piece and wash again until clean.
  3. Marinate it in lemon soda.
  4. Arrange the shrimp in a bowl and pour lemon soda.
  5. Let it sit for around 10 minutes before cooking.
  6. Serve the buttered shrimp on plate.
